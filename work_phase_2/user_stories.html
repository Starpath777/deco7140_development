User Stories

35-year-old Australian RTS Fan

Act as a 35-year-old man who loves real-time strategy games and wants to find a partner to battle with.

How do you feel when you win or lose a game?

If I win through strategy, I feel very accomplished and might write some analysis.

If I lose due to my own mistakes, I reflect and learn; if it’s due to system or bugs, I feel it loses the competitive essence.

How do you feel when special situations occur (like disconnection or rule exploitation)?

Disconnection: I feel it’s an unfair failure and hope for a reconnect function.

  • Rule exploitation makes the game boring. I expect developers to quickly rebalance it.
  • If an opponent gives up or trolls, the game feels meaningless, I might switch platforms.

What features are essential?

  • Tactical replay system to analyze decisions.
  • Stable server with reconnecting capability.
  • Tactical matching system to find players with similar strategies.

What part of the platform helps you most?

Replay analysis and platform stability are the most valuable for me.

18-year-old UK Girl (LOL Fan)

Act as an 18-year-old girl from the UK who is a huge fan of League of Legends and wants to find teammates and show off her record.

How do you feel when you win or lose a game?

If I win, I feel awesome and love to share screenshots on Twitter or Instagram.

If I lose because of bad teammates, I get super angry and might report them.

How do you feel when special situations occur (like teammates quitting or server issues)?

If a teammate quits mid-game, I feel like it's a waste of time.

Toxic chat or arguments make me want to leave the game immediately.

If the server crashes and my record isn't saved, I get really disappointed.

What features are essential?

  • Match history system to show MVP count and win rate.
  • Smart matchmaking to avoid unfair matches.
  • Social system to add friends or follow players and even have fans.

What part of the platform helps you most?

I love the match history display. If I can show my stats on social media, I’ll definitely stick with the platform.

25-year-old Chinese FPS Gamer

Act as a 25-year-old guy from China who loves FPS games and wants to find teammates to rank up.

How do you feel when you win or lose a game?

I’m focused on winning. If I win because of solid teamwork and skill, it feels great.

If I lose because of hackers or imbalance, I get very frustrated.

How do you feel when special situations occur (like cheating or poor matchmaking)?

Hackers ruin the game—I expect a strong anti-cheat system.

If the matchmaking is too one-sided, it feels like a waste of effort.

What features are essential?

  • Powerful anti-cheat system to ensure fairness.
  • Ranked matching to pair players with similar levels.
  • Voice and quick chat features to help with communication.

What part of the platform helps you most?

Having reliable anti-cheat and fair matchmaking is what keeps me playing.
