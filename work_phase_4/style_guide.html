Style Guide

Welcome to the style guide for the Urban Food website created as a concept for the Connected Communities Initiative. In this guide, you'll find the design language and component styles that inform the look and feel of our pages.

Button Styles

Primary Button

主按钮使用高对比度背景色；悬停时背景色加深，按下时略微缩放，下陷效果；禁用时降低不透明度并禁止交互。这样能让用户明确感知“可点”、“已点”、“不可点”。
The primary button uses a high-contrast background; on hover the background darkens, on press it slightly scales down with a depressed effect; when disabled it reduces opacity and disables interaction. This clearly communicates “clickable,” “pressed,” and “unclickable” states to users.

Secondary Button

次按钮使用边框 + 透明背景；悬停时边框颜色加深或出现阴影；按下时内边距微调；禁用时移除边框并降低对比度。
The secondary button features a border with a transparent background; on hover the border color deepens or a shadow appears; on press the padding adjusts slightly; when disabled the border is removed and contrast is lowered.

Icon-only Button

纯图标按钮内嵌 emoji/SVG，使用 aria-label 确保屏幕阅读器用户也能理解其含义。悬停和按下同样会提供视觉反馈（如边框、阴影或颜色变化）。
The icon-only button embeds an emoji or SVG and uses aria-label to ensure screen reader users understand its purpose. Hover and press states also provide visual feedback (e.g., border, shadow, or color change).
